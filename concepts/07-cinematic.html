About · The studio

Small studio. Wide range.

VXVO is an independent studio. The remit is broad on purpose — productivity tools one week, an arcade game the next, a translator the week after. The throughline is taste, polish, and the discipline of shipping.

Everything is designed and built end-to-end: identity, frontend, backend, mobile, and the boring infrastructure that keeps it running.

  • WebNext.js, React, TypeScript, edge runtimes
  • MobileNative & cross-platform on iOS and Android
  • AIClaude, GPT, structured generation, agents
  • BackendSupabase, Postgres, Cloudflare Workers
  • DesignIdentity, product UI, motion, illustration
  • GamesOriginal titles, sandbox systems, arcade
