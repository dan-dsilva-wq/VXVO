iii.
The studio

VXVO is a small studio for software that lasts — productivity tools one week, an arcade game the next, a translator the week after. The remit is broad on purpose; the throughline is taste, polish, and the discipline of shipping.

Everything is designed and built end-to-end: visual identity, frontend, backend, mobile, and the boring infrastructure that keeps it running. The studio works in tight loops with users and ships small, often.

  • WebNext.js, React, TypeScript, edge
  • MobileiOS & Android, native & cross-platform
  • AIClaude, GPT, agents, structured output
  • BackendSupabase, Postgres, Cloudflare Workers
  • DesignIdentity, product UI, motion, illustration
  • GamesOriginal titles, sandbox systems, arcade
